About

Octo Dodger is a fun game that can be played using any guesture that one prefers.

At first let us calibrate to the control of the spaceship.

  • Make a gesture or posture that you want for the spaceship to move left and click on “move left” a couple of times.
  • Make a gesture or posture that you want for the spaceship to move right and click on “move right” a couple of times.
  • Make a gesture or posture that you want for the spaceship to not move and click on “no movement” a couple of times.
  • Click on “Train”.

And that's it now using the same moves you can control the ship, enjoy the game :)
